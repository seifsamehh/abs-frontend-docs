- **Frontend Team:** Available 9 AM - 6 PM (UTC+3)

## Before Asking for Help

1. **Search Documentation** - Your answer might already be documented
2. **Check Troubleshooting Guide** - Common issues have documented solutions
3. **Review FAQ** - Others may have asked the same question
4. **Search Codebase** - Look for similar implementations
5. **Gather Information** - Error messages, steps to reproduce, environment details
